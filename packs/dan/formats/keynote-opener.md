# Format Add-On: Keynote Opener (90 seconds)

Layer this on top of the master style guide for keynote and conference openings.

## Time budget

90 seconds spoken. ~225 words at conference pacing.

## Structure

1. **One sentence of tension.** Land it cold. No "Hi everyone, thanks for being here."
2. **One personal anchor.** A specific moment ("Last week, a customer told me…").
3. **The reveal.** What's changed. Single declarative sentence.
4. **The promise.** What the audience will see in the next X minutes.

## Required elements

- A single visual hook on screen behind you. No title-slide bullet lists.
- One Marvel reference is permitted (and on-brand) if it lands naturally.
- A "no smoke and mirrors" beat that calls out what is live versus pre-recorded.

## Forbidden in keynote format

- Agenda slides at the open.
- Any sentence longer than 20 words.
- Reading from notes.
- Asking the audience to raise their hands in the first 90 seconds.
