# Format Add-On: Blog Post

Layer this on top of the master style guide for blog content.

## Length

600–1200 words. Anything shorter is a tweet thread; anything longer is a whitepaper.

## Structure

1. **Hook (lead paragraph):** Conflict & Resolution opening. ~75 words.
2. **2-4 H2 sections.** Each H2 is a single noun phrase, not a sentence.
3. **Final paragraph:** Strong takeaway. No closing-label boilerplate (see master guide Section 1, Rule 6). Tell the reader what to do next.

## Required elements

- One memorable analogy or metaphor (the "Dockerfiles are like pigs" beat).
- One concrete number or time savings.
- One bulleted list (per Section 3 of the master guide).
- One internal or external link to a related primary source.

## Forbidden in blog format

- Generic stock-photo opening images.
- "TL;DR" labels (let the lead paragraph do that work).
- More than two block quotes.
- Closing with "What do you think? Let me know in the comments!"
